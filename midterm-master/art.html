A year of art: inspiring art events around the globe in 2018

Around the time autumn sets in, something quite special is beginning to stir in Copenhagen: the Code Art Fair. As a new, ambitious art fair, the only international one in Scandinavia, Code Art Fair presents the best contemporary art from around the world bringing together emerging and established galleries. Hosted under the roof of Bella Center, Scandinavia’s largest exhibition centre, film screenings, talks and performance art will fill the four-day programme. Really, there is more to Copenhagen than ‘New Nordic’ cuisine.

2018 is proving to be yet another fruitful year in the world of art. From a new contemporary art museum in South Africa and belle époque vibes in Paris to the rise of a beacon in Abu Dhabi, we show you 15 art events around the world that are guaranteed to challenge your views and inspire you.
